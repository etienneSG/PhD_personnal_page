I am a PhD student in Optimization and Operational Research at Argon Consulting and the CERMICS. With my research advisors Vincent Leclère and Frédéric Meunier and with Argon Consulting, we are working on arbitrage between cost and flexibility in Supply Chain.

Argon Consulting is a independent French consultancy firm specialised in Supply Chain and located in Paris metropolitan area (France). Its clients are big companies, investment funds and middle-market companies in the sector of industry, distribution and services as well as public organisations. CERMICS is a laboratory of École des Ponts ParisTech located in Paris metropolitan area (France). The scientific activity of CERMICS covers several domains in Scientific Computing, Modelling, Optimization and Operation Research.
